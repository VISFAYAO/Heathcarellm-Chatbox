Canadian Mental Health Association – Self-care

Regular, quality self-care:
- improves mental health and self-worth
- increases optimism
- reduces anxiety and depression symptoms :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Simple daily self-care activities:
- get a good night’s rest
- eat balanced meals
- take a bath
- spend time with pets
- spend time in nature (walking, gardening, etc.)
- do physical activity (workout, stretching, sports)
- meditate or practice mindfulness
- get creative (art, writing)
- socialize with supportive people
- seek therapy when needed :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Self-care also includes setting healthy boundaries:
- limit screen time
- maintain regular work hours :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
